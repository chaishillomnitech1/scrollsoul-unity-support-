## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:
- Clarifying standards of acceptable behavior
- Taking appropriate and fair corrective action
- Enforcing this Code of Conduct with compassion and wisdom
- Modeling exemplary behavior aligned with our principles

Maintainers have the right and responsibility to:
- Remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct
- Temporarily or permanently ban contributors for inappropriate behavior
- Make final decisions on conduct issues while remaining accountable to the community

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All project spaces (repository, issues, pull requests, discussions)
- Official community channels and events
- Public spaces when representing the project
- Private communications related to project matters

## Enforcement

### Reporting

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by:
- Opening a confidential issue (if the platform supports it)
- Contacting project maintainers directly
- Using the project's designated reporting channels

All complaints will be:
- Reviewed and investigated promptly
- Kept confidential to protect reporters
- Handled with fairness and respect for all parties

### Consequences

Project maintainers will determine consequences based on the nature and severity of the violation:

**1. Correction**
- **Impact**: Minor violation or misunderstanding
- **Consequence**: Private, written warning with clarity about the violation and expected behavior

**2. Warning**
- **Impact**: Moderate violation or pattern of minor violations
- **Consequence**: Formal warning with consequences for continued behavior, including temporary restrictions

**3. Temporary Ban**
- **Impact**: Serious violation or sustained inappropriate behavior
- **Consequence**: Temporary ban from project interaction for a specified period

**4. Permanent Ban**
- **Impact**: Severe violation, harassment, or sustained pattern of violations
- **Consequence**: Permanent exclusion from project participation

### Appeals

Decisions may be appealed by:
- Contacting project maintainers with additional context
- Requesting review by a different maintainer
- Demonstrating corrective actions taken

## Restoration and Growth

- Second chances for those who demonstrate genuine change
